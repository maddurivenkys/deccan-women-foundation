About US

Deccan Women Foundation Charitable Trust, all started with motivation to help people during covid pandemic time. During pandemic due to lock down many people suffered without job and it became hurdle for many of us to have basic needs. We took a small step forward to support these poeple by providing food and groceries. We stand for spreding peace and and harmony in the society irrespective of cast and creed and community, we also believe in helping and supporitng the people in need by whatever possible means hence Deccan Women Foundation Charitable Trust established to fulfill the needs of needy.

We are a group of likeminded people with soft hearts and open mind who joined together with the sole purpose of bringing peace and harmony in the lives and hearts of the underprivileged.
